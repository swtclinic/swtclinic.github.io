Provided TREATMENTS

Physicians, tuners, and nursing staff provide comprehensive Chinese medicine health services:
Acupuncture, Cupping, Scraping, TUINAT Therapy and TDP Lamp.

Acupuncture

Acupuncture is a TCM methodology that treats patients by manipulating hair-thin needles inserted into specific points on the body. World Health Organization (WHO) has recognized that acupuncture is clinically proven for more than 60 conditions such as sciatica, arthritis, depression, insomnia, and musculoskeletal injuries.

Cupping

Cupping is a tool for using cans as a tool to generate negative pressure by means of burning, pumping, etc., so that it can be adsorbed on the body surface, which can dehumidify the cold, dredge the meridians, resolve the qi and activating blood circulation, reduce swelling and pain, and remove the poison and diarrhea. Adjust the body's yin and yang balance, relieve fatigue, and enhance physical fitness.

Scraping

Scraping is guided by the theory of traditional Chinese medicine meridians and acupoints, through special scraping instruments and corresponding techniques. It has the functions of regulating blood circulation, promoting blood circulation, relieving collaterals and relieving evil spirits. Especially suitable for painful diseases, bone and joint degenerative diseases such as cervical spondylosis, recovery of frozen shoulder; for cold, fever, cough and other respiratory diseases can be used in clinical combination with cupping; for acne, chloasma and other damage-causing diseases Combined with acupuncture, puncture and bloodletting, etc.; also suitable for the prevention and treatment of sub-health, chronic fatigue syndrome and other diseases.

TUINAT Therapy

Massage refers to the treatment of meridians, acupoints, push, take, lift, pinch, and sputum on the human body by hand. It has the functions of restoring physical strength, reducing fatigue, enhancing human blood circulation, improving human body's disease resistance, regulating organs, beauty, weight loss, disease prevention and treatment, and prolonging life.

TDP Lamp

The magic lamp is a heat-induced acupuncture point or a specific part of the body caused by the moxibustion material made of Ai Ye, and the physiological and biochemical functions of the human disorder are adjusted by exciting the activity of the gas. It has the functions of meridian regulation, regulation of immunity, and qi and collaterals.
